Poetry

বাংলা কবিতা

আমার সোনার বাংলা
আমার সোনার বাংলা, আমি তোমায় ভালোবাসি।
চিরদিন তোমার আকাশ, তোমার বাতাস,
আমার প্রাণে বাজায় বাঁশি।

ও মা, ফাগুনে তোর আমের বনে,
ঘ্রাণে পাগল করে, মোরে...
ও মা, অঘ্রাণে তোর ভরা ক্ষেতে,
আমি কী দেখেছি মধুর হাসি।

আমার সোনার বাংলা, আমি তোমায় ভালোবাসি।

English Poem

The Road Not Taken – Robert Frost

Two roads diverged in a yellow wood,
And sorry I could not travel both
And be one traveler, long I stood
And looked down one as far as I could
To where it bent in the undergrowth.

I shall be telling this with a sigh
Somewhere ages and ages hence:
Two roads diverged in a wood, and I—
I took the one less traveled by,
And that has made all the difference.

Compositions

My Country

Bangladesh is a small but beautiful country in South Asia. It is full of rivers, green fields, and natural beauty. People here are simple and hardworking. Our culture is rich and we are proud of our independence.

Village Life

Village life is simple and peaceful. People live close to nature. They work in the fields, grow crops and live happily with less facilities but more unity and peace of mind.

City Life

City life is modern and fast. People get many opportunities like education, hospital, job and entertainment. But it is also noisy, crowded and sometimes stressful compared to village life.

Student Life

Student life is the best time of life. It is the time to study, learn discipline, and prepare for the future. A good student uses time properly and respects teachers and parents.

A Journey by Train

Last month I had a journey by train from Dhaka to Chattogram. The train passed through green fields, rivers, and villages. It was very enjoyable to see the natural beauty of Bangladesh from the window.
