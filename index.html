2. Poetry

Bangla Poem – বসন্তের আভাস

বসন্ত আসে ফুলের ডাকে,
পাখির গানে ভোরের বুকে,
সবুজ পাতায় রঙের খেলা,
মন ছুঁয়ে যায় আলো মেলা।

হাওয়ায় মেশে স্মৃতির ছোঁয়া,
শীতের শেষে প্রাণের জোয়া,
বসন্ত বয়ে আনে আশা,
জীবনে ভরে নতুন ভাষা।

English Poem – Whispers of Dawn

The night retreats, the stars grow dim,
A gentle light begins to brim,
The dawn whispers a hopeful song,
A place where dreams and hearts belong.

Through silver mist the world is new,
A promise bright, a sky of blue,
With every ray the dark has gone,
We rise to greet the shining dawn.

3. Study (10 Compositions & Paragraphs)

1. My School

My school is one of the best places in my life. It is a place where I gain knowledge, learn discipline and build my future. The teachers are friendly and caring. There is a library, a playground and a computer lab. I enjoy reading books and playing with friends. My school inspires me to become a good citizen. I feel proud of my school.

2. A Rainy Day

A rainy day is a day of joy and sorrow. The sky remains covered with clouds. Rain falls continuously. Roads become muddy and waterlogged. People use umbrellas and raincoats. Students sometimes enjoy holidays. Farmers become happy because rain helps crops grow. Children make paper boats and play. Though it creates trouble, rain is a blessing for nature.

3. My Best Friend

Everyone needs a true friend. My best friend is honest, kind and helpful. He always supports me in my studies and games. We share our secrets and help each other in difficulties. A good friend is like a treasure in life. I feel lucky to have a best friend who encourages me to do good things and avoid bad habits.

4. Importance of Reading

Reading books is a great habit. It increases our knowledge, improves our language and develops our mind. Books give us information about science, history, literature and culture. Reading makes us wise and thoughtful. It also entertains us and removes stress. Students should read storybooks, newspapers and educational books regularly. Reading helps to build a bright future.

5. Save the Environment

The environment is our home. It gives us air, water and food. But pollution is destroying it day by day. Cutting trees, using plastic and smoke from factories are harmful. We must plant trees, recycle waste and use clean energy. If we protect nature, it will protect us. Saving the environment is saving our own lives.

6. My Hobby

Hobby means something we do for pleasure in our free time. My hobby is gardening. I have a small garden in front of my house. I plant flowers and vegetables. I water them daily. Watching flowers bloom makes me happy. Hobby refreshes our mind and makes life enjoyable. Gardening also teaches me patience and love for nature.

7. Computer: Friend or Foe

Computer is a great invention of modern science. It helps us in study, business, medical science and communication. But using it too much can harm our health. Some people misuse it for crimes. We should use computers wisely. If used properly, it is our best friend. If misused, it can be our worst enemy.

8. A Visit to the Zoo

Last week I visited the zoo with my parents. I saw many animals like lions, tigers, deer, elephants and monkeys. Birds of different colors were flying in cages. The zoo was full of visitors. I learned about the habits of animals. Visiting the zoo is both enjoyable and educational. I wish to visit again.

9. Festivals of Bangladesh

Bangladesh is a land of festivals. We celebrate Pahela Baishakh, Eid, Durga Puja and Independence Day. People wear new clothes, prepare delicious foods and visit relatives. Festivals bring joy and unity among people. They also reflect our culture and tradition. Festivals make our life colorful and meaningful.

10. How to Study Effectively

Studying effectively is important for success. We should make a timetable and follow it. Concentration is necessary. Reading with understanding is better than memorizing. Writing practice helps us remember. We should ask teachers questions when in doubt. Proper sleep and good health also help in study. If we study regularly and smartly, success will come.
